How to use MedLib.

Each card summarizes a medication with a visual cue and a quick caution note.

Study with layers

Start with the image to anchor recognition, then scan the class, route, and onset info. These are the cues most students need for quick comparisons.

Connect to clinical context

Use the chips for typical use and duration. The note line reminds you of a key caution or monitoring tip so you can connect mechanism to safety.

Keep it safe

This library is for learning and memory aid only. Always verify dosing, interactions, and contraindications with primary references.
