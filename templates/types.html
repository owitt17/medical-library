Medication classes, simplified.

Open a card for quick class snapshots and examples.

Antibiotics

Fight bacterial infections by inhibiting growth or killing organisms.

  • Penicillins: Amoxicillin
  • Macrolides: Azithromycin
  • Cephalosporins: Cephalexin
Analgesics & NSAIDs

Reduce pain, fever, and inflammation.

  • Analgesic: Acetaminophen
  • NSAIDs: Ibuprofen, Naproxen
Cardiovascular

Control blood pressure, lipids, and clot risk.

  • ACE/ARB: Lisinopril, Losartan
  • Statins: Atorvastatin
  • Antiplatelets: Aspirin, Clopidogrel
Endocrine & Metabolic

Support glucose control and metabolic balance.

  • Biguanides: Metformin
  • Insulins: Glargine
Respiratory & Allergy

Open airways and reduce allergic responses.

  • Beta-2 agonists: Albuterol
  • Antihistamines: Cetirizine, Diphenhydramine
GI & Mental Health

Manage reflux, ulcers, and mood disorders.

  • PPI: Omeprazole
  • SSRIs: Sertraline, Fluoxetine
